Sunshine Resort Customer Analysis

As a Business Analyst working with Sunshine Resort, I spearheaded a comprehensive data analysis intiative aimed at optimizing guest satisfaction and revenue generation. Our Project stood out for its diverse clientele, attracting guests from various parts of the world, including Europe, North America, and South Africa. Leveraging sophisticated analysis techniques in Microsoft Excel, we meticulously examined reservation data encompassing bookings, cancellations, customer preferences, and revenue streams. Through detailed analysis and visualization, we uncovered a notable churn rate among these customers. These insights were pivotal in guiding strategic decision-making within the resort. Collaborating closely with our team, we tailored interventions that led to a commendable 10% decreaase in the resort's churn rate. This experience not only showcased our proficiency in leveraging data-driven methodologies but also highlighted the effectiveness of Microsoft Excel as a tool for analysis and visualization in the hospitality sector.

CrossCredit Data Analysis Project

At CrossCredit, I significantly contributed to optimizing credit offerings by analyzing customer behavior and credit utilization patterns using Microsoft Power BI. Our team explored key metrics such as attrition rates, gender distribution, card types, and relationship counts by tenure. We identified income categories with the highest attrition, assessed the impact of dependents on spending, and analyzed revolving balances on activity. We also evaluated transaction volumes, average credit limits, assistance frequency, and utilization ratios. Our comprehensive analysis yielded actionable insights, enhancing customer satisfaction, streamlining operations, and supporting growth. By developing strategic recommendations, we improved credit approval processes and customer retention, demonstrating the power of data-driven approaches in the financial sector.
